Maintenance and Sustainability

Maintenance and Sustainability refer to the strategic approach of preserving and enhancing the long-term functionality, value, and impact of assets, investments, and systems.

Maintenance involves proactive and ongoing efforts to ensure that operations, infrastructure, or portfolios remain in optimal condition, minimizing risks, disruptions, or losses over time.

Sustainability focuses on aligning business practices with responsible economic, environmental, and social principles — ensuring that present success does not compromise future needs. It encompasses ethical investing, resource efficiency, and long-term value creation.
